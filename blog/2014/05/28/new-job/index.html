New Job

2014-05-28

I recently had the opportunity to take my love of programming from being a hobby to being a career. I jumped at the chance.

I am now in my 3rd week of the new job and have yet to go to work. Instead, I meet up with fellow nerds and geek out on programming. I have survived my first agile sprint and resolved two introductory issues with the codebase; one in PHP (Symfony 2 with Doctrine) and the other in JavaScript (ExtJS).

I have much to learn in my new career and I look forward to every second of it.
